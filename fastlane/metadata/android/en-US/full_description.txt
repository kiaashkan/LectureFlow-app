LectureFlow is an Android app for the LectureFlow web service — an AI-powered lecture transcription tool designed for pharmacy and science students.

Features:
- Upload audio recordings of lectures
- Get full transcript powered by Whisper AI
- Extract formulas, technical terms, and key points
- Download results as TXT
- Automatic dark/light mode based on system setting

This app is a WebView wrapper for https://lf.kiaashkan.workers.dev
